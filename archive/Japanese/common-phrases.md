## Basic Phrases

### Greetings

- **おはようございます** (Ohayou gozaimasu) - Good morning (formal)
- **おはよう** (Ohayou) - Good morning (casual)
- **こんにちは** (Konnichiha) - Hello / Good afternoon
- **こんばんは** (Konbanha) - Good evening
- **おやすみなさい** (Oyasuminasai) - Good night

### Polite Expressions

- **ありがとうございます** (Arigatou gozaimasu) - Thank you (formal)
- **ありがとう** (Arigatou) - Thank you (casual)
- **どういたしまして** (Dou itashimashite) - You're welcome
- **すみません** (Sumimasen) - Excuse me / Sorry
- **ごめんなさい** (Gomenasai) - I'm sorry

### Useful Phrases

- **はじめまして** (Hajimemashite) - Nice to meet you (first time)
- **よろしくお願いします** (Yoroshiku onegaishimasu) - Please take care of me
- **トイレはどこですか** (Toire wa doko desu ka) - Where is the bathroom?
- **水をください** (Mizu wo kudasai) - Water, please

## Numbers

- **ゼロ** (Zero) - 0
- **一** (Ichi) - 1
- **二** (Ni) - 2
- **三** (San) - 3
- **四** (Shi/Yon) - 4
- **五** (Go) - 5
- **十** (Juu) - 10
- **百** (Hyaku) - 100

Keep practicing these phrases regularly!
