---
title: "Morning Reflections"
date: "2026-03-04"
tags: ["poem", "morning", "nature", "reflection"]
---

The dawn breaks softly through the window,
A gentle reminder of the new day ahead.
The world awakens slowly,
While I sit with my thoughts.

Coffee steam rises in spirals,
Each breath carries possibility.
The quiet before the storm,
When silence still holds meaning.

Birds sing their ancient songs,
Indifferent to human concerns.
They know something we've forgotten—
How to live in the present moment.

I watch the light grow stronger,
And feel the day pulling me forward.
Yesterday fades like morning mist,
Tomorrow waits with open hands.
