---
title: "Code and Coffee"
date: "2026-02-28"
tags: ["poem", "code", "programming", "life"]
---

Lines of code like poetry on screen,
Each character a brushstroke.
The cursor blinking—
A heartbeat of creation.

Coffee grows cold as hours pass,
The world outside forgotten.
Problems solved, only to discover
New mysteries waiting beneath.

There's beauty in the logic,
Structure in the chaos.
A problem's solution is art
When it flows from the mind correctly.

The joy of the debug,
Finding that one missing semicolon—
How small a thing can break so much,
How small a fix can make things whole again.
